Research Profile

I am a Master’s graduate in Physics (Applied Electronics) from the University of Kerala (CGPA 9.58/10), working at the intersection of experimental condensed matter physics, quantum materials, and nonequilibrium statistical mechanics.

My primary research goal is to experimentally investigate correlated and topological quantum systems using precision low-temperature transport, magneto-transport, and spectroscopic techniques. I am particularly motivated by experimentally intensive environments involving cryogenic platforms, custom instrumentation, and quantitative data-driven modeling.

My work integrates rigorous analytical modeling (generalized Fokker–Planck frameworks, non-Markovian dynamics) with experimental spectroscopy and transport-oriented methodologies.

Research Interests

  • Low-temperature quantum transport in correlated materials
  • Topological and symmetry-driven electronic phases
  • Magneto-transport and Hall measurements
  • Light–matter interaction and ultrafast spectroscopy
  • Nonequilibrium stochastic dynamics and memory effects

Research Experience

Generalized Fokker–Planck Equation for Active Ornstein–Uhlenbeck Particles

University of Kerala · Jun 2025 – Sep 2025 · Supervisor: Dr. Mamatha Sahoo

Developed an analytical and computational framework for nonequilibrium stochastic systems incorporating inertia, memory kernels, and external fields. Derived measurable transport observables from microscopic dynamics and implemented numerical simulations in Mathematica and Python. Manuscript under review in Physical Review E.

Spectral Insights into Carbonaceous Chondrites Origins

Physical Research Laboratory (PRL), Ahmedabad · Mar 2025 – Jun 2025 · Supervisor: Prof. Kuljeet Kaur Marhas

Performed quantitative mid-infrared spectral analysis using FTIR measurements and archival space-based spectroscopy. Developed Python-based spectral similarity frameworks and thermal modeling tools to correlate CV chondrites with main-belt asteroids. Manuscript under review in Icarus.

Design & Fabrication of Discrete Transistor-Based Logic Circuits

University of Kerala · Feb 2022 – Apr 2022

Designed and fabricated transistor-based logic gates using BC547 devices, including PCB layout, circuit optimization, and systematic validation.
